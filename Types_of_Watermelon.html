Types of Watermelon

Seeded Watermelons

Seeded Watermelons are typically larger than other types of watermelons weighing between 15-45lbs.

Seedless Watermelons

Seedless Watermelons are the most commonly grown type of Watermelon because of a high demand. They typically weigh between 10-25lbs. Many people believe seedless Watermelons to be genetically modified but they are actually just hybrids.

Mini(personal)Watermelons

Personal Watermelons have thinner rinds and more flesh per pound(because of thin rind)

Yellow and Orange Watermelons

These types of watermelon are typically less known, but are sweeter than basic watermelon.
